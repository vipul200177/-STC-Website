Academic Conference

April 10th-12th

Details

Problem Satatement

SAC aims to provide an opportunity for Bachelors’ and Masters’ students from all disciplines to present their research in a professional academic setting. Here, Student Research refers to any scholarly or creative activity ranging from scientific experimentation, service-learning, and literary criticism, to case-study design, and artistic expression. All houses will submit abstract entries. The abstracts will be invited in several domains. Professors will shortlist from amongst the abstracts and the selected abstracts will then be invited to submit 4 papers which will be presented in front of a panel of professors during the ICTC. There will be some participation points as well as some individual awards.


Judging Criteria

Each Research Paper will be given a TOTAL of 25 MINUTES in which 15 MINUTES will be given for PRESENTATION and 10 MINUTES for QUESTIONS FROM THE PANEL.

SAC invites at most 6 abstracts from each house. Based on selected abstracts, an invitation will be sent to invite one of the authors of the abstract to present in SAC as a part of the team from their respective house. For the first 3 papers, each paper presented by a particular house in the SAC shall earn them 30 event points while the 4th paper shall earn 35 points. More than 4 abstracts can get accepted but the event points for the presentation will be capped at 125.
The decision of the judges shall be FINAL.


Timeline

  • Abstract Submission: Before March 22, 2020
  • Final presentation: At ICTC (April 10-12, 2020)
